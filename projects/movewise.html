About the Project

Overview

MoveWise was developed as my Final Year Project to help users perform exercises correctly using AI posture tracking while also providing personalized workout plans, hydration tracking, and chatbot assistance.

It combines modern technologies with an intuitive UI to deliver a seamless fitness experience.
