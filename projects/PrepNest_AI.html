Overview

Preparing for tech interviews is often overwhelming due to endlessly long reference articles and video modules. PrepNest AI streamlines this lifecycle by chunking down domain concepts into high-impact review summaries.

By blending structured local notification rules, optimized NoSQL client caching architectures, and clean fluid design primitives, the app ensures that preparation remains highly accessible, continuous, and offline-first.
